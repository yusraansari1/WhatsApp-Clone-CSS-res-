Whatsapp Bussiness is an Android app which is free to download, and was built with
the small business owner in mind. With the app, businesses can interact with
customers easily by using tools to automate, sort, and quickly respond to messages. If
you're a large business, learn more about the WhatsApp Business API.

END - TO - END ENCRYPTION

Security by Default

Some of your most personal moments are shared on WhatsApp, which is why we built
end-to-end encryption into the latest versions of our app. When end-to-end encrypted,
your messages and calls are secured so only you and the person you're communicating
with can read or listen to them, and nobody in between, not even WhatsApp.
